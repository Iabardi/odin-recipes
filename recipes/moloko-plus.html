Moloko Plus

Straight from the Korova Milk Bar

The infamous cocktail adopted by Alex and his droogies. According to Mr. DeLarge, a glass of Moloko plus “would sharpen you up and make you ready for a bit of the old ultra-violence.”

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces rum
  • 1/2 ounce Del Maguey Crema de Mezcal
  • 1/2 ounce Bénédictine
  • 3 ounces cardamom-infused milk
  • 1 barspoon simple syrup
  • 2 dashes orange bitters, Angostura
  • 2 ounces sparkling wine
  • a nice amount of you know what

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients (except sparkling wine).
  2. Add ice and shake until chilled.
  3. Strain into a highbball glass.
  4. Add 2 ounces chilled sparkling wine.
  5. Add desired amount of you know what.
